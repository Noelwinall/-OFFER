1. Debenture

Debenture is used for supporting school development such as increasing educational resources, supporting new
developments and innovations in the �eld of education, and enhancing campus environment, facilities,
equipment upgrade, etc. The purchase of a debenture is mandatory for all new Early Childhood Education (Two’s
Programme and above), Primary and Secondary students. The debentures are non-interest bearing and fully
refundable when students leave the school.

2. Sibling Policy

We have a sliding scale sibling policy for parents who have more than one child studying full-time in Yew Chung
International School - Hong Kong.
The youngest child: full payment

The second child and onwards: less 10%

3. School Uniform

School uniform is mandatory. There is a summer uniform, a winter uniform and a uniform for Physical Education
and sporting activities. Except for shoes and socks, which can be purchased from local stores, all uniform items
need to be pre-ordered. Please call +852 2339 6928 for more details.

4. School Lunch Fees

School lunch is optional in Primary and Secondary sections and not applicable in Early Childhood Education
section. Primary and Secondary students may bring their own packed lunch by arrangement. Please call
+852 2336 5015 for more details.

5. School Bus Fees

School bus service is optional for Early Childhood Education section and Secondary. The Primary section runs
a mandatory bus scheme (any exception needs to be granted by the school).
Please call, for ECE section,

+852 2336 8255 or

+852 2578 1178 for Primary and Secondary sections.

6. Scholarships

Scholarships are awarded to Secondary students who demonstrate excellent academic achievement or special
talent in other categories. Successful recipients need also to maintain high behavioural standards, a commitment
to serving the school, critical thinking, proven leadership skills and team spirit.
For more information please contact

+852 3975 6820 or email
